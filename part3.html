PROBLEM:

The problem with representative democracy is that it makes politics a job, so we get a political class. It's very easy for the political class to become insular. We see this in many countries, where a small number of families or dynasties vie for power - consider the two Bush presidents, for example, or the Clintons.

Once we have a political class, it becomes concerned with safeguarding and extending its own privileges. When this happens at the expense of the good of the rest of the nation, it becomes a huge problem.

While seats at the state and congress level are meant to best represent people below them, often times the elected party is swayed by financiers rather than representing the public.

MISSION:

Placing a high ranking official in the government, who agrees to give away his final say in voting based on votes cast in a secure voting application, thus giving people true representation in Government.

Operation Free The Bird is kickstarting a domino effect, we will become a beacon for the replication of this sentiment in order to reach a point at which point the US Government will be represented directly by the people of America.

True representative democracy aka Liquid Democracy is what our founding fathers of this nation would have created if the technology existed when our nations democratic system was set up.

The only reason we did not set up true representative democracy from the get go is that our communication speed for information was that of a horse.

Thus, Representatives that represented their state was at that time the purest form of democracy possible without causing a massive headache in collaboration with thousands of people in a room shouting.

After over two thousand years, large corporations and an insular political class has formed around the power that the government represents. Delegates are supposed to represent the majority they tend to fall to the power law of financiers. This has led our nation to become divided leaving a majority of voices unrepresented and unheard.

Rather than representatives LOOSELY attempting to represent the vote of the public and failing, with todays technology the public can now literally be represented.

If you are thinking that this means every single person needing to vote towards every bill, that would of course be ineffective as most people do not have time to research each new proposal that is referred to as “Direct Democracy”.

Instead, we reach a more fluid democracy by breaking down the false barriers we previously placed based on technological restrictions, to allow any person to be a representative, and for any person to change their vote at any time. That means that at any moment, I can give my voting power to any individual in the world who I want to represent me, and my vote will be cast based on their best judgement.

What if the entire political system ran on true democracy that everyone could vote on through their phones?

Obviously getting political leaders to give up their POWER of representation to give true representation to the nation over is not going to happen by their will.

Instead we will enact this revolution seat by seat of the senate and the state. With the first election that completes this operation acting as an initial dominos to kickstart this inevitable and great future where corruption from centralized power is brought to its knees.
